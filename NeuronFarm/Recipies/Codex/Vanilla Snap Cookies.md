## Ingredients 

QTY | Desc
---:|---
185g |unsalted butter, chopped
220g | caster (superfine) sugar
1½ | teaspoons vanilla extract
375g |plain (all-purpose) flour
1 | egg
1 | egg yolk

## Method
1. Place the butter, sugar and vanilla in
a food processor and process until smoot
2. Add the flour, egg and extra egg yolk
and process until a smooth dough forms,
3. Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured
surface and divide in half
4. Roll each piece out between sheets of
non-stick baking paper until 5mm thick
5. Refrigerate for 10 minutes or until firm
Preheat oven to 160°C (325°F).
6. Remove the top sheets of baking paper
from the dough. Using a 6cm round cookie cutter, cut shapes from the dough, re-rolling as necessary.
7. Place the rounds on lightly greased baking
trays lined with non-stick baking paper and bake for 12-14 minutes or until golden.
8. Allow to cool on trays for 10 minutes
before transferring onto wire racks to cool.
