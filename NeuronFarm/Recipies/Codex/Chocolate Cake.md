*Makes 2 8in cakes or 1 10in cake*

## Ingredients

QTY | Desc
---|---
450g |white sugar
350g |flour
60g |cocoa flour
3 tsp |baking soda
1 tsp |salt
1 tbsp |instant coffee
240ml | hot water
240ml |milk
180ml |vegetable oil
1 tbsp |vinegar
1/2 tbsp |vanilla

## Method

1.  Preheat oven to 175 C
2.  Using a stand mixer and whip attachment, combine sugar, flour, cocoa, salt and baking soda in the mixer and turn on low speed. This will sift the ingredients.
3.  Add espresso powder to the water and set aside.
4.  Add vinegar to the milk and set aside.
5.  Add vanilla to the oil.
6.  While the mixer is running on low, add the oil mixture, milk mixture, and half of the water mixture.
7.  Mix until incorporated and no lumps are found.
8.  Add the last bit of water and blend until fully incorporated.
9.  Fill pans and bake for 30-45 minutes, checking the cakes at 30 minutes for doneness.

Preheat oven to 350 F and spray pans with nonstick cooking spray.
