## Ingredients

QTY | Ing
---|---
100g | Chocolate, chopped
1 tbsp | olive oil
1 tbsp | vegetable oil (or double olive oil)
¼ tsp | cinnamon
¼ tsp | sea salt flakes

## Method
1. Combine everything into a microwave safe bowl and microwave in 20 seconds increments, stirring after increment until melted. Allow it to cool a little.
2. Pour it over everything you like, or use it to coat frozen fruits and other treats.
