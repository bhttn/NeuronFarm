## Ingredients

QTY | Desc
---:|---
 100g | Sourdough Starter
 450g | Strong White Bread Flour
 8g | Salt
 310g | Water (at room temperature)

## Method
### Timing Guide
#### Day 1 
- Feed starter in the evening 
#### Day 2 
- Mix dough + 30 minutes rest 
- 1st Fold (12 folds) + 2 hours rest 
- 2nd Fold (6 folds) + 2 hours rest 
- 3rd Fold (6 folds) + 1 hour rest 
- Pre-shape + 1 hour rest 
- Final shape + refrigerate overnight 
#### Day 3 
- Bake
