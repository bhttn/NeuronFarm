Serves 4
## Ingredients
750g Pilton Valley Premium Pork belly, skin removed and cut
into 2-3cm squares
½ cup cornflour
2 teaspoons smoked paprika
2 teaspoons ground cumin
2 teaspoons sea salt flakes
½ teaspoon finely ground white pepper
¼ teaspoon finely ground black pepper
vegetable oil, for deep frying
lemon wedges and chilli sauce, to serve

## Method
1. Combine cornflour, paprika, cumin, coriander, sea salt,
white pepper and black pepper in a bowl. Add Pilton
Valley Premium Pork belly to spice mixture and toss until
lightly coated.
2. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Deep-fry pork
in batches for 4-5 minutes or until golden and crisp.
Transfer to a tray lined with paper towel and keep warm.
Repeat with remaining pork.
3. Serve pork with lemon wedges and chilli sauce.
