## Ingredients

QTY | Ing
---|---
4 oz. | butter
1 cup |chopped boiled Bunya Nuts
2 cups | S.R. flour
1 cup |coconut
1| egg
2 | tablespoons honey
2 | teaspoons milk.

## Method

1. Sift flour, add coconut, rub inbutter. 
2. Add beaten egg, honey and milk. 
3. Turn into stitf dough.
4. Add cup Bunya nuts.
5. Place teaspoons of mixture on to greased tray.
6. Bake in moderate oven 10-15 minutes, or until golden brown.
