## Ingredients

QTY | Ing
---|---
160g | icing sugar
280g | smooth peanut butter
80g | unsalted butter
1 tsp | vanilla extract
80ml | single cream

## Method
1. Placing icing sugar, peanut butter, butter and vanilla in a mixing bowl
2. Beat on medium for 4 minuted till well combined
3. Add the cream and beat for 1 minute until light and fluffy
4. Spread
