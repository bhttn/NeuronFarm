## Ingredients
### Base

QTY | Ing
---|---
200g | shortbread or digestive biscuits
100g | unsalted butter
1 tbsp | golden syrub

### Filling

QTY | Ing
---|---
100g | milk chocolate, chopped
100g | dark chocolate, chopped, (plus extra shaved chocolate to garnish)
1 tsp | vanilla extract
2 tbsp | pure icing sugar, sifted
200ml | thickened cream, plus extra to serve
. | Cocoa powder, to garnish
 
## Method
### Base
1. Place the biscuits in a food processor and pulse to form fine crumbs. 
2. Place the butter and golden syrup in a saucepan over low heat, stirring, until butter melts
3. Pour into processor and pulse to combine. 
4. Press the biscuit mixture into the base and sides of six 10cm loose-bottomed tart pans. Chill until required.
### Filling
1. Melt the milk and dark chocolate together in a heatproof bowl over a saucepan of simmering water (don't let the bowl touch the water). 
2. Remove from heat, stir until smooth, then allow to cool for 5 minutes. 
3. Stir in the vanilla and fold in the icing sugar .
4. Whip cream until soft peaks form, then fold into the chocolate mixture. 
5. Pour into the tart pans and chill for at least 2 hours until the chocolate filling is set.
### To Serve
Drizzle the plates with melted chocolate, top tarts with extra whipped cream and sprinkle with shaved chocolate and cocoa powder . Serve with strawberries .
