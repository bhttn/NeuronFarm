Can leap up to 2m

The red-tailed phascogale does not drink, as its water is metabolised through its food

Alternative names for the species include red-tailed wambenger, # Red-tailed phascogale, kenngoor (the latter in the [Nyoongar language](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nyoongar_language "Nyoongar language")),[[4]](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red-tailed_phascogale#cite_note-sprat-4) and red-tailed mousesack.

found in dense and tall Vegetation containing Wandoo (_[Eucalyptus wandoo](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eucalyptus_wandoo "Eucalyptus wandoo")_) and the [rock sheoak](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rock_sheoak "Rock sheoak")
