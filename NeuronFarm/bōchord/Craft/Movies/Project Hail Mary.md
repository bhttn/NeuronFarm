Can two space bros work out what is eating stars before the earth freezes? 

The plot and story are typical Weir garbage however as a vehide for Ryan Gostig it is perfect. The ship is gorgeous and rocky the alien is a perfect foil for Grace. Come for the visuals, stay for the banter and leave the science at home.
