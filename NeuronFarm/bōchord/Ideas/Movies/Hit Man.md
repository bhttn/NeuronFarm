## Review
A fake hit man for the police falls for one of the marks. Things start to spiral as the ripples grow. 

Charisma of the leads carry the movie. Subtle growing confidence of Gary being shown was a nice touch. Ending did not feel deserved and left a sour taste in an otherwise enjoyable story.
