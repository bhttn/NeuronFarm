## Review
Roz seeing the world that she was built for longs for her real home, the island. 

The craft and care in the writing are echoed in the story and themes. A joy to read. A study of what it means to fit in and how to deal with the pain that discovery can bring to those that are left behind. All hiding in a cracking adventure story.
