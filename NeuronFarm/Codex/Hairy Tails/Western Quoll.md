During the day it seeks refuge in hollow logs or earth burrows as dens
most active around dusk ([crepuscular](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crepuscular "Crepuscular")) when it hunts.
It possesses an ability to obtain most of its water from its food,
