# Recap
## Issue 1
Everyone wakes up on a flight with amnesia, when they disembark one person is missing from the flight. A true crime author starts investigating the missing passenger years later, seems to have her own memory issues? Following a lead in Mexico a CIA agent saves her from an attack by *immortals*.
A village has the same sickness as the people on the flight, the kids are unaffected. All the adults are compelled to make pots with a symbol on them from Zanzibar. 
## Issue 2
They are followed to Zanzibar by immortals. Following a lead she meets another journalist who writes streams of conscious that might be the future? Meeting Maru is too much for her and she jumps off the balcony. Perrier was a twin who could write suggestive stories with her sister. 
Is the mysterious man in the coffee shop the narrator?
## Issue 3
The narrator appears to be Henry Lyme the missing passenger.
