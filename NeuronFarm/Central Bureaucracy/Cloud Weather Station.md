### HOW TO PREDICT THE WEATHER
**Clear liquid:** Bright and clear weather.
**Small dots in the liquid:** Humid or foggy weather.
**Threads of crystals at the top:** Windy weather.
**Cloudy liquid:** Cloudy, possible thunderstorms and rain.
**Large flaky crystals:** Cloudy skies, snow in winter.
**Crystals at the bottom :** Frost may be coming.

### Poison 
KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. READ SAFETY DIRECTIONS.
CAMPHOR 18.45g/205g. FLAMMABLE.
TOTAL NET WEIGHT 195g TO 205g.


### First Aid
 For advice, contact a Poisons Information Centre (e.g. phone Australia 13 11 26; New Zealand 0800 764 766) or a doctor at once. Urgent hospital treatment is likely to be needed. If swallowed, do NOT induce vomiting. Avoid giving milk or oils.
