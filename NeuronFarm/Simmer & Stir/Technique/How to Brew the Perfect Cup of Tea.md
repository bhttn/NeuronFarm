## Tea Tips

- **Re-brew** green, oolong, and white tea leaves up to three times.
- Use a **heaped spoon** for larger leaves and a **flattened spoon** for broken leaves.
- **Warm your teapot and cup** with a little boiling water before steeping — this keeps your tea hot.
- **Store leaves** in an airtight container in a cool, dark place.
